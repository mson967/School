About Our Islamiyya Program

Our Islamiyya program is designed for children aged 6-12 years, divided into six levels. We focus on building a strong foundation in Islamic knowledge and practice through engaging lessons and activities.

Program Features

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Regular assessments and progress reports
  • Islamic environment and role models
  • Extracurricular activities and competitions

About Our Tahfiz Program

Our Tahfiz program is designed for students who wish to memorize the entire Quran. We provide a structured environment with qualified teachers who have Ijazah in Quran recitation.

Program Structure

  • Daily memorization targets with revision
  • Regular testing and evaluation
  • One-on-one attention from teachers
  • Focus on understanding and application
  • Preparation for Ijazah certification
